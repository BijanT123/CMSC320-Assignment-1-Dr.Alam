Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git makes it easy to collaborate on a project. For our semester-long project, this is useful as we will be working in groups. Additionally, git is something most students have used before, so most will have familiarity with it.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can easily be applied to statistics because data science works with data and usually performs statistical operations on this data. This is the basis of our semester-long project.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging is nondestructive and safer than rebasing, and is better when working in a group. However, rebasing is much faster and cleaner, and better when working alone.
